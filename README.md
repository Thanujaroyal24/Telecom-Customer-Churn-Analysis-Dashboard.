# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecommunication companies. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and business growth. This project analyzes customer behavior, identifies churn patterns, and provides interactive dashboards that help businesses understand customer demographics and the major factors contributing to customer churn.

The project is developed using **Microsoft Power BI** with an interactive report consisting of three pages:
- Home Page
- Customer Dashboard
- Churn Analysis Dashboard

The report enables business users to explore customer information through interactive filters, KPIs, charts, and navigation buttons.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Telecommunication companies often struggle to identify the reasons behind customer churn. Without proper analysis, organizations cannot develop effective customer retention strategies.

This project aims to:

- Analyze customer demographics.
- Monitor customer distribution.
- Identify factors influencing customer churn.
- Measure revenue loss due to churn.
- Provide an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

# 📊 Key Business Insights

The dashboard helps identify several important business insights:

- Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn rates.
- Customers without Tech Support are more likely to leave.
- Customers without Online Security tend to churn more frequently.
- Electronic Check payment method is associated with higher churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure have a higher probability of leaving.
- Fiber Optic internet customers experience relatively higher churn.
- Customer demographics help understand different customer segments.
